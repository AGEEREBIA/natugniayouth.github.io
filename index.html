NATUGNIA YOUTH ASSOCIATION/ONLINE

Location: Natugnia, Upper East Region, Ghana

Motto: READY TO SERVE

About Us

Founded in 2023 by Atuah Isaac and Ageere Dominic Agumah, NATUGNIA YOUTH ASSOCIATION/ONLINE was born out of a desire to unite the youth of Natugnia, both home and abroad. Starting with a WhatsApp group, our community has grown to over 500 members. Many others joined in to power the movement that is now making an impact in the community and beyond.
